Winter Suites Hotel, Owerri

Experience warm hospitality in the heart of Owerri. Winter Suites blends contemporary comfort with local charm to create a restful stay for business and leisure travellers alike.

Established to set a new standard for boutique accommodations, Winter Suites began as a family-owned venture focused on attentive service and well-appointed rooms. From day one we set out to offer exceptional value — inviting spaces, modern amenities, and personalised service that make every guest feel at home.

Today, our hotel welcomes guests from across the region. Whether you're visiting for a conference, a weekend getaway, or a special celebration, our team ensures a seamless stay with comfortable rooms, reliable dining options, and thoughtful touches that reflect the spirit of Owerri.

Rooted in hospitality and driven by care, Winter Suites continues to grow while staying true to our founding promise: to deliver memorable experiences through genuine service, clean and safe environments, and a commitment to guest satisfaction.

Our Roots

Born from a passion for thoughtful hospitality, our roots are in personalised service and attention to detail. What started as a small property has matured into a beloved local address.

Our Promise

We promise comfort, cleanliness, and consistent service — so every guest can relax, recharge, and enjoy the best of Owerri.
